About Me

Award winning basketball statistician with a long history of working in the sports industry with previous experience in traditional engineering and manufacture. Professionally new to the world of data, keen to work from the ground up and gain the necessary skills to make a career. Professional experience with Power BI, Python, R, SQL and Excel. Sheffield Hallam University graduate with BSc Sports and Exercise Technology and MSc Sports Engineering. Currently studying for an MSc in Data Analytics. Previous intern with the Sports Engineering Research Group (SERG) focused on data analysis and insights for top level British national governing bodies. Current Data Analyst and Scrum Master at the British Business Bank (BBB) helping the UK's economic development bank grow small businesses and companies.

Journey

My journey to becoming a data analyst has not been linear. I started out studying traditonal engeering, switched to sport and now I work in finance! Along the way I have earned a Bachelors degree (BSc (Hons) Sports Technolgy, first class), a Masters degree (MSc Sports Engineering, Merit) and stared another Masters (MSc Data Analytics, Distinction (predicted)). View my timeline below to learn more about my career.

2025

Founded NextPlay Stats! A project I have considered since 2020, finally made a start. ...

2024

After two successful years as a data analyst, I have learned a lot about Power BI, SQL and Excel. I am in charge of my own development and aim to build robust reporting for various areas of my company. Accepted a degree apprenticeship with SHU to study for my second Masters degree, MSc Digital Technology Solutions Specialist (DTSS) Data Analytics. Became a Scrum Master, leading daily Scrum calls with a development team. Current qualification from Cloud Academy, stuyding for Professional Scrum Master certification.

2022

Became an intern with SERG after my work with my mentor. Developed skills further, becoming more confident data analysis. Completed my Masters degree working near full time at the fast food restaurant. Secured first professional role as a data analyst in finance.

2021

Finished Bachelors degree. Took interest in final year data management module, using gathered basketball statistics data to build a small database for coaches to gain a better understanding of the statistics. Developed skills in Python and database usage through Microsoft Access. Graduated with a first class degree. Started masters degree in Sports Engineering. Took up the opportunity for a data mentorship by the software development lead for the Sports Engineering Research Group (SERG, formerly CSER). Through the mentorship, developed skills in Python, SQL, PowerBI, Excel and general data management.

2020

Work placement with the Centre for Sports Engineering Research (CSER). Helped with various projects with a focus on design engineering and product development. Compeleted SHU Leadership Award. Recognised for abilities as a statistican by being nominated for and winning Yorkshire's statisician of the year for the previous season. Recognised for my ability to do the job alone when there are two separate roles. Consistently scored above 90 % accuracy using the statistics software.

2019

First year of university was successful. Contiuned to work three jobs whilst producing high quality coursework. Developed skills in research tecniques, mechanics, human science, design and professional development. Started to learn MATLAB. Became a department rep for the Academy of Sport and Physical Activity at SHU. Joined a univeristy society committee. Accepted a promotion at to train crew at the fast food restaurant.

2018

Became more confident in abilities and graduated top of the class with near perfect marks in sport, recieving an award for my efforts. Spent time with the computing department learning about Python. Accepted an unconditional offer from SHU to study BSc (Hons) Sports and Exercise Technology. Received a high achivement scholarship for my A-Level results. Whilst working part time as a statistican and fast food crew member, began third role as a SHU student ambassador.

2017

Began to study big data analysis in mathematics. Started work at a fast food restaurant whilst studying. Considerd applications for University with an interest in completing the Sports Engineering masters degree at Sheffield Hallam University (SHU).

2016

After completing first year of A-Levels, made the decision to switch disciplines to study sport at the new UTC based at the Olympic Legacy Park (OLP). I initally struggled with the new field. Others on the course had prior experience studying sport, creating a disadvantage.

2013 - 2015

Left secondary school to join the new University Technical College (UTC) to study Advanced Engineering and Manufacture. Recieved an award for Outstanding Achivement in Engineering. Continued engineering journey at UTC studying Mathematics and Physics A-Levels alongside Engineering. Became a Basketball England trained statistican for my local basketball club.
